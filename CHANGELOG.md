# v1.0.0 (The Beginning) - 6 March 2026

### ✨ Core Features
- **Task Management** — Add, edit, and complete tasks with a clean, distraction-free interface
- **Estimated Time** — Set hour/minute estimates per task to plan your day
- **Live Timer** — Start a focus session directly from a task; actual time is tracked automatically
- **Floating Timer Panel** — A minimal always-on-top panel that stays visible across all spaces and full-screen apps while you focus
- **Session Dates** — Tasks are scoped to the day they were created, keeping your list clean day-to-day
- **Offline** — 100% offline, you control your data.

### 📊 Stats \& Insights
- **Metrics Dashboard** — View completed tasks and total focused time across Today, This Week, and This Month
- **Focus Sessions** — Every completed task logs a focus session for historical tracking

### ♻️ Misc
- **Auto-update** — Auto checks for updates with Sparkle.
